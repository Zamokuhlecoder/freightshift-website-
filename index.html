About FreightShift

We are a South African based freight forwarding company that specialises in moving your cargo swiftly and securely across borders. We combine cutting-edge technology with hands-on expertise to shift your shipments with precision, on-time delivery and in-transit care so you can focus on your business.

From air and sea freight to customs clearance and warehousing, we are your reliable partner in keeping the supply chain moving forward. We also focus on national logistics services within South Africa and neighbouring countries.
